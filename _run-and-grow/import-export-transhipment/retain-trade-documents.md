## Retain your Trade Documents

Generally, you are required to retain the relevant supporting documents relating to the purchase, import, sale or export of the goods for a period of 5 years from the date of the permit application approval.<br>

These documents can be stored as physical hardcopies or asÂ [images](https://www.customs.gov.sg/files/businesses/customs-guide-on-keeping-and-maintaining-records-in-image-system.pdf){:target="_blank"}{:rel="noopener"}. You are required to produce these supporting documents to Singapore Customs upon request.
