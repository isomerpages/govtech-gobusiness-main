## Finnex Management Consulting

Finnex Group of Companies is an All in One Business Sustainability & Globalisation Management Consultancy. Our founder & lead consultant, Kei Chia is a Registered Management Consultant (RMC), making him a certified EnterpriseSG Consultant and Entrepreneur of the Year Award (EYA) 2022 winner. Since March 2025, we are also a COSIRI Assessor for ESG readiness, particularly for manufacturing industry, certifying them.

Our team of consultants specialises in:
1. Business Strategy Development
2. ESG Framework & Reporting
3. IP Strategic Roadmap
4. Globalisation Strategy & Roadmap
5. Finance Management & Optimisation
6. Human Capital Development
7. Business Matching 
8. IP Registration (Local & via Madrid Protocol)
And many more.

Our project works are mostly enabled via EDG Grant and MRA Grant by EnterpriseSG.

Do check with us on your eligibility if you are unsure.
