## Claudian Caucus

Claudian Caucus is Asia's leading IP strategy and consulting firm that provides business advisory services to technology companies and investors. Claudian Caucus helps clients manage IP risk by identifying, evaluating and acquiring IP assets related to business, corporate and product development.

Claudian Caucus boasts a depth of expertise in novel technologies ranging from biotechnology, life sciences and biopharma therapeutics to cutting-edge semiconductor and AI software. Claudian Caucus' clients include (a) leading and emerging companies from Asia and US, (b) investors in IP assets and IP-rich companies, (c) leading educational institutions. Claudian Caucus is led by Jonas, the first Singapore law clerk in the US Federal Courts. Some representative work highlights include (i) the Nobel-prize winning CRISPR technology and various biopharma therapeutics, (ii) novel semiconductor production methods, (iii) AI software patents, (iv) fuel cell technologies, (v) AgriTech methods.
