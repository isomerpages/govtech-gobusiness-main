## Viering, Jentschura & Partner LLP

Viering, Jentschura & Partner (VJP) Singapore is part of a more than 250 member strong international Intellectual Property (IP) law firm headquartered in Munich, with branch offices in D&Uuml;sseldorf, Dresden and Taiwan in addition to Singapore. Our offices do not only share a single name but we also function as a single cohesive corporation that strives to build a Truly Single Firm presence around the world. Our Singapore branch office has been established since 1997 and, more than 25 years, has been developed to a full-service IP prosecution firm in this country.
