## aiCARE Shield Managed Security

## Overview

**Description**

NTC Integration Pte Ltd was incorporated in 1994, with 30 years of proven track record, to provide quality Cybersecurity & Managed Services to its clients.

aiCARE Shield is a Managed Detection & Response solution to help SME mitigate the challenges faced to secure their organization against constant threat of Malicious Virus, Malware and Ransomware.

It offers pro-active monitoring of Endpoint devices and Servers to detected, analyze and remediate cyber threats keeping organization safe.
