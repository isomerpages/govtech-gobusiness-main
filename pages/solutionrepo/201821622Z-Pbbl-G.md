## Pebble

## Overview

**Description**

Pebble is a media infrastructure platform designed to empower SMEs with AI-driven image and video generation capabilities. By leveraging Pebble's media AI tools, SMEs can easily create customised marketing content tailored to their brand needs. The platform includes built-in analytics tools that help SMEs track and understand the performance of their generated media . Pebble is ideal for SMEs looking to improve their speed to market for marketing campaigns.
