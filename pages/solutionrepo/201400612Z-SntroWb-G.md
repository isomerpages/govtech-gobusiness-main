## SentroWeb

## Overview

**Description**

SentroWeb is a cloud-based application that corporate secretarial firms need to be productive in their compliance and corporate secretarial work.  With powerful workflow engine and search engines, the solution includes accurate and reliable AML/CFT data source from reputable data vendors.  The solution provides a simple workflow that guides the user in information collection, document generation, event reminders, AML/CFT screening and risk assessment. The company is also ISO 27001 certified.
