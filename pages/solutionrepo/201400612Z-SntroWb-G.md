## SentroWeb

## Overview

**Description**

SentroWeb is an Anti-Money Laundering/Countering the Financing of Terrorism (AML/CFT) screening and Customer Due Diligence (CDD) system. It uses AML/CFT data from Dow Jones or Acuris, covering worldwide Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs), individuals and entities with past records of serious crimes, and global (including United Nations) and domestic sanctions. The system provides alerts through ongoing monitoring, notifying users of new profiles matching previously screened names.
