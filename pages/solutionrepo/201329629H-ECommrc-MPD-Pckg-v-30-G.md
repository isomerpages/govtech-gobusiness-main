## E-Commerce MPD Package Version 3.0

## Overview

**Description**

MediaPlus Digital provides E-Commerce web solution designed for SMEs in Singapore. The platform aims to enhance digital presence and increase sales by expanding customer reach through an intuitive interface. It offers features for seamless online browsing, shopping, and purchasing. The solution includes capabilities for operational efficiency, scalability, reporting, customer relationship management, and multi-channel integration, all intended to support SMEs in their digital commerce efforts.
