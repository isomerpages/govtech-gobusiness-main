## Confirmation.com

## Overview

**Description**

Confirmation is a web-based platform offering a secure and efficient way for auditors to confirm financial information with banks, customers, vendors, legal firms. 

Key benefits include improving the overall quality and reliability of financial audit procedures, enhanced security, reduction of fraud risk, improved efficiency and transparency, tracking and reporting capabilities, auto-reminders and more.

In Singapore, we have 34 validated banks and departments live on our network.
