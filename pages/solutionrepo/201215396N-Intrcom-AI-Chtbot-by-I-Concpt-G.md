## Intercom AI Chatbot by I Concept

## Overview

**Description**

Intercom AI Chatbot is an AI agent developed by Intercom for frontline customer support. It can be integrated with existing platforms or used with Intercom's customer service platform. The AI Chatbot aims to handle a wide range of support tasks, offering an AI-driven approach to customer service.
