## Time Access HRMS

## Overview

**Description**

Time Access HRMS is a cloud-based human resource management system that integrates various HR modules with security hardware. It offers functionality for attendance, payroll, leave, claims, benefits, and appraisal management, alongside biometric clock and door access control features. The system includes a mobile app with an employee self-service portal and can be integrated with other business software solutions.
