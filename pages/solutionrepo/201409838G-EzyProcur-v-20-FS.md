## EzyProcure Version 2.0

## Overview

**Description**

EzyProcure is a one-stop procure-to-pay electronic business solution which acts as a bridge between buyers and suppliers. Since going live in 2016, it has become the top choice for businesses to simplify procurement and automate workflows, with seamless integration to ERP and accounting systems. 

EzyProcure provides an intuitive user interface with multilingual capabilities. It's proprietary OCR technology helps save businesses up to 70% in time and resources in the daily procurement, payment
