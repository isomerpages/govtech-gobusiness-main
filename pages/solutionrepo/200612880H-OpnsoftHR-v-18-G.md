## OpensoftHR Version 18

## Overview

**Description**

Opensoft is a provider of Payroll and Human Resources Management System (HRMS) solutions, with operations based in Singapore and Malaysia. OpensoftHR today comprises of Payroll, Leave, Claims, Appraisal and Mobile Time Attendance modules, equipped with employee self-service functions, a real-time dashboard that features a visual snapshot of the workforce, and a unique data export function to serve workforce analytical needs.
