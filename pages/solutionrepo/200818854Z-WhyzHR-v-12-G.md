## WhyzeHR Version 12

## Overview

**Description**

Whyze Solutions is a HR & Payroll solution provider based in Singapore since 2008. Combining HR experiences from various industries, we deliver reliability and flexibility for HR to meet evolving organizational needs. To date, we support over 3000 companies in Singapore from various industries including startups, SMEs, nonprofit organizations to MNCs. With WhyzeHR, you have a full cloud HRIS that combines payroll, benefit, attendance, appraisal, training, onboarding, and employee engagement.
