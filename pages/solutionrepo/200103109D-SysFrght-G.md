## SysFreight

## Overview

**Description**

SysFreight is a freight management solution that provides functionality for freight, transport, and logistics operations. It utilizes technology to computerize and simplify processes, accounting, and administration in the freight industry. The system aims to streamline operations for businesses in the logistics sector.
