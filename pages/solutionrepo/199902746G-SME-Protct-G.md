## SME Protect

## Overview

**Description**

ST Engineering's AI Enabled Threat Elimination and Response (AETHER) offers end-to-end cyber protection for your business. Designed from the ground up for small and medium businesses and powered by Palo Alto's Cortex XDR, AETHER provides your business with real-time protection against malicious applications, ransomware, and other cyber threats. With a fully managed service, AETHER lets you leave security monitoring to the experts, allowing you to focus on what really matters: Your Business
