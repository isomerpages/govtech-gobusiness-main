## recruitSG Applicant Tracking System (ATS)

## Overview

**Description**

recruitSG's Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is a comprehensive and user-friendly platform designed to streamline your recruitment process. 

Effortlessly post job openings, screen candidates, and manage the hiring pipeline. The ATS also simplifies interview coordination by enabling seamless scheduling and centralising interview feedback from multiple stakeholders. Gain access to recruitment metrics, providing valuable insights to help you optimise recruitment strategies to hire top talent.
