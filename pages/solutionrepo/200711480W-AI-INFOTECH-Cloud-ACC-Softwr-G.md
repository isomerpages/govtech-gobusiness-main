## (A.I.) INFO-TECH Cloud Accounting Software

## Overview

**Description**

Info-Tech Cloud Accounting Software offers a secure, cloud-based solution for businesses of all sizes to manage their accounting needs effectively. It features e-invoicing, bank reconciliation, customizable template, inventory & asset management, budgeting & financial reporting. It compliant with IRAS and automatically updates GST changes. Accessible on mobile devices, it enables users to oversee finances and streamline operations from anywhere, helping them make informed business decisions.
