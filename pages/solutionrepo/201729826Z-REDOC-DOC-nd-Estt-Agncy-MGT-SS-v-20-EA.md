## REDOC Document and Estate Agency Management SaaS Version 2.0

## Overview

**Description**

Redoc is a real estate agency management SaaS platform in Singapore. It helps salespersons close deals faster and simplifies documentation and workflow for agency businesses. Features include smart documents with digital signing verified on SingPass, sales dashboard, integrated digital real estate transactions management, CRM module with lease management tools, AML and KYC screening, HR management with incentive scheme tools, and invoice management with accounting platform integrations.
