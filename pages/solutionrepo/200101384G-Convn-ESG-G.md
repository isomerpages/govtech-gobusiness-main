## Convene ESG

## Overview

**Description**

Convene ESG is a digital sustainability reporting and carbon management solution that utilises AI to automate ESG data collection and workflows for greater validation and audibility, while reducing human effort and errors. It allows enterprises to measure and track their carbon emissions with country-specific, sector-specific, and global emission factors. Enterprises can generate robust reports aligned with global ESG frameworks and regulatory provisions (SGX, GRI, TCFD, and IFRS S1 and S2 etc.)
