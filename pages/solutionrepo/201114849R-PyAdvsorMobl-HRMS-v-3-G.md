## PayAdvisorMobile HRMS Version 3

## Overview

**Description**

PayAdvisorMobile HRMS is a Human Resource Management SaaS with proven capabilities to handle conventional office-based and mobile workforce effectively. PayAdvisorMobile HRMS is available on Web and Mobile platforms. Modules include Personnel, Time-Attendance, Payroll, Leave, Claim and Performance Appraisal. Comprehensive dashboards and reports provide instantaneous data insights to the HR team. Optional add-on modules include Recruitment, Onboarding, Offboarding, Training, KPI and Rostering.
