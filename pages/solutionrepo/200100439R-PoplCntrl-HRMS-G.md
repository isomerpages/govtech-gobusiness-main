## PeopleCentral HRMS

## Overview

**Description**

PeopleCentral HRMS is a cloud-based platform for human resource management. It offers features for various HR processes including payroll, leave, claims, attendance, scheduling, training, and performance management. The system includes functions for employee onboarding, management dashboards, workflow automation, alerts, announcements, and employee self-service. It also provides a mobile application, analytics capabilities, and options for integration and customisation.
