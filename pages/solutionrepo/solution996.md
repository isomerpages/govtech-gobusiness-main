## Dining Butler system

## Overview

**Description**

Dining Butler provides an end to end solution for F&B that covers the following modules
1) mobile ordering
2) POS operations
3) kitchen management
4) waiter management
5) backend item management
6) CRM
7) inventory 
8) reports
