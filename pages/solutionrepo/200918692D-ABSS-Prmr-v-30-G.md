## ABSS Premier Version 30

## Overview

**Description**

Integrated accounting digital solution with nationwide E-invoicing system with modules Financial Statement Statement of financial position, comprehensive income, and cash flow), Account Receivable/Payable, Billing and Invoicing,  General Ledger, Purchase Order and Sales Order and Data Analytics capabilities.
