## SQL Account Version 5

## Overview

**Description**

SQL Account includes full range of business Accounting features including AR, AP, GL, Inventory, Banking, GST, Reporting. It has user-friendly interface to help SMEs perform daily operation, organise and analyse finance data for accurate reporting. SQL Account is customisable to suit specific business workflow and needs. It also supports multi-currencies, project & departmental costing, multi-warehouses and InvoiceNow.
