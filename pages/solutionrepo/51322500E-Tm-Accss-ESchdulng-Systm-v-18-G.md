## Time Access E-Scheduling System Version 1.8

## Overview

**Description**

TIME ACCESS E-Scheduling System is a Time Attendance and Rostering software developed from 30 years of market experience. The Integrated Cloud Solution combines Web Application/Mobile application/Biometric Hardware. Manage staff with e-timesheet reports and duty rosters from any location. Track IN/OUT attendance from the Mobile App with GPS Geo-location and Geo-fencing features. The solution can be integrated to any existing Business Software, or further upgraded to a Payroll/HRMS software.
