## Eurostop Omni-Channel Retail Management System Version 10

## Overview

**Description**

This omnichannel retail management solution integrates Point-of-Sale (POS) with inventory management and CRM modules. It offers features for retailers' sales operations including automated discounts for promotions, membership management with customisable discounts and points, and reporting on sales and stock movements. The system provides real-time inventory control and insights, aiming to support retailers in managing their operations and customer relationships.
