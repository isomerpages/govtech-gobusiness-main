## CARDDIO Version 2

## Overview

**Description**

CARDDIO is a cloud-based solution for sales and marketing, compatible with iOS, Android, and web platforms. It provides a centralised system for lead management, campaign tracking, and marketing automation. The software offers management real-time visibility of sales activities, allowing for identification of performance gaps. CARDDIO aims to help businesses improve operational efficiency and productivity through its various features.
